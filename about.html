About us

Rooted in nature.
Guided by purpose.

Vetriver was born from a simple belief: the answers to healthier living have always existed in nature.

For centuries, Vetiver has quietly purified the air, refreshed water, strengthened the land, and cooled homes across India. We exist to bring that timeless wisdom back into modern life through meaningful handcrafted creations.

Our name

Our name reflects our purpose.

Vetri means Victory.
Ver means Root.

Together, Vetriver stands for prosperity that grows from healthy roots: cleaner air, fresher water, living soil, and homes connected to nature.

Grown with care, for the long term.

Today, we work with more than 2,000 contract farmers across different regions of India, ensuring responsible cultivation while supporting sustainable farming practices and the livelihoods of the communities who grow it. Every root we buy is a commitment to the land it came from, and the hands that raised it.
